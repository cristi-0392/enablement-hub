    📦 Solution Export¶

    This section outlines how to export your solution for reuse or deployment in other environments.


    📂 Export your Copilot Studio solution

    Begin by confirming you are in the Development environment, which contains the most up-to-date version of the solution. Then, select it as shown below.

    Click on Export Solution from the top menu.

    In the side panel that opens, select Publish again before exporting to ensure all recent changes are included in the solution.

    Then, click Next

    Althought this might not be the case, during the pre-export validation, some required components may be identified as missing. To ensure the solution functions correctly when imported, it is recommended to add these components by selecting them and clicking Add to solution.

    Back to the export wizard, a version number will be suggested, which you can adjust if needed. It is highly important that you select Unmanaged for your solution export as this ensures it remains editable upon import into another environment. Leave the "Run solution checker on export" selected, for a final validation. Then, click Export

    The side panel will collapse and a ribbon informing you that the solution is currently being exported will display. Depending on the size and assets contained in the solution, it can take a few minutes until it's exported successfully.

    Once the provisioning of your unmanaged solution package is complete, you'll be prompted to download it locally. Click on Download

    Your solution is now downloaded and saved as a .zip file to your local machine, typically in the Downloads folder or another location based on your broswer settings.

    As a final step, navigate to the designated folder in the Copilot Studio Teams General channel and upload your solution there.




    ⏭️ For future reference: Importing your unmanaged solution in an environment

    In the future, once your organization provides an environment for importing the solution, ensure you are in the correct environment and select Import Solution from the top menu.

    An import dialogue box will be displayed. Click on Browse

    Select your unmanaged solution and click Open

    After selecting the .zip file, click Next. A validation process will then evaluate the solution.

    Lastly, you will be presented with the known details of your solution. Click Import

    Once initiated, the side panel will close, and a notification ribbon will display the progress of the import.

    Upon completion of the import process, the solution will be available in the selected environment as an unmanaged solution.
